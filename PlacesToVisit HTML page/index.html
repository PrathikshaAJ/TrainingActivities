Dakshina Kannada district is located in the state of Karnataka in India, with its headquarters in the coastal city of Mangalore. It is part of the larger Tulu Nadu region. The district covers an area nestled in between the Western Ghats to its east and the Arabian Sea to its west.It is a popular tourist destination due to its beautiful beaches, gorgeous mountain ranges, temples, and vibrant culture. Yakshagana, a magnificently costumed dance theatre form; Kambala, the farmer pastime of racing buffaloes, Kori-katta (cockfighting); and Bootha Kola (spirit worship) are all well-known in Dakshina Kannada.
